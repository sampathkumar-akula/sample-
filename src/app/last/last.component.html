Digital Classrooms

A digital classroom is a boon to the present generation and is a fool proof method for better understanding and concept clarity at all levels right from nursery to grade 10. So we have equipped all our class rooms with touch screen digital boards along with projectors, desktop and speakers as it is an aid for both the teacher and the student which inculcates interest and enhances the class room learning activity to the maximum extent possible..
